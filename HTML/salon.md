# Making of A PRD for Salon Shop 

## Objectives
Build a website for a saloon shop in order to give details of work done in saloon.

## Scope 
- online booking for customer. 
- Advance online booking.
- not real time tracker for free slots
- not online payement through website.
- types of service provided with extended details.

## p1
- details about haircut and beards.
- pictures of haircutting done in salon.
- online booking for time slot

## p2
- online realtime tracker for slots 
- online advance payments.
